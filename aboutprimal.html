About Primal Customs

At Primal Customs, every month brings a fresh burst of creativity. We release four exclusive designs that are printed on high-quality, plain t‑shirts. Our customers choose not only the design they love but also the color that suits their style. This dynamic approach ensures that your wardrobe is always updated with the latest trends and artistic expressions.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Primal Customs emerged from a passion for art and fashion. What began as a small idea quickly evolved into a vibrant brand known for its innovative printed t‑shirts. Our journey is driven by creativity, quality, and the desire to empower individual style.

2023 – Inception

Primal Customs was founded with the vision to redefine wearable art, launching with a limited edition collection that celebrated creative expression.

2024 – Growth & Recognition

With our innovative approach and commitment to quality, we expanded our collection and gained recognition as trendsetters in the fashion industry.

2025 – Leading the Trend

Today, we continue to set trends by releasing four new designs every month, ensuring our customers have access to the latest in art-inspired fashion.

Our Release Cycle

Every month, Primal Customs releases four new designs that are expertly printed on plain t‑shirts. Customers can choose from a variety of designs and customize their selections with their preferred color options.

  • Four exclusive designs released monthly.
  • Printed on premium, high-quality t‑shirts.
  • Customers select both the design and their preferred color.

Our Vision & Mission

We envision a world where fashion meets art, and every shirt is a canvas for self-expression. Our mission is to empower individuality through high-quality printed t‑shirts that fuse innovative design with exceptional craftsmanship.
